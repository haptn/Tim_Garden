Description

Sunflower

  • Very tall, some species can grow to a height of 300cm or more

  • Are used as food plants (their seeds are used as human food)

  • Phytosterols in seeds promote healthy cholesterol levels

Rose

  • Roses have acquired cultural significance in many societies

  • Used for commercial perfumery and commercial cut flower crops

Tulip

  • Large, showy and brightly coloured, generally red, pink or yellow

  • Are adapted to steppes and mountainous areas with temperate climates

  • Flowering in the Spring, they become dormant in the Summer
